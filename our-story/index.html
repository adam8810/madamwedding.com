Mallory Vaughn & Adam Booth

October 20th, 2012

Our Story

The Beginning

Adam and Mallory attended Oklahoma Christian University together, but it wasn’t until their senior year that they realized how much they had in common.

In September of 2010 Mallory posted on facebook complaining about how her laptop was giving her so much trouble and Adam responded offering to help. Later that evening Adam made a “house call” to help her with her computer and that was when they really hit things off. After being with Mallory for a short time that evening, he ran back to his apartment to grab tools to help her with her sick computer. Although he didn’t normally talk about girls to his friends, he couldn’t help but share how beautiful he thought she was to everyone in the room.

He hurriedly ran back to her apartment, and spent the rest of the evening with her talking and comparing their music playlists, and then movies they loved. They created a list of movies to watch together as a way to continue seeing each other.

Nearly a month later, Mallory asked Adam to attend a friend’s wedding back in her hometown of Grapevine, Texas and he happily agreed! After watching a movie that ended in the early morning, Adam asked Mallory to be his girlfriend and they’ve been so happy ever since. They’ve been together for 4 years, 9 months, and 27 days.

The Ring

In October while Adam was visiting Mallory back in Texas, he took Mallory ring “browsing”, with the intention of figuring out what she liked and didn’t like when it came to engagement rings, which was something completely foreign to him. To his surprise (and relief) Mallory chose “the ring” and pointed it out to him on multiple occasions. It’s a good thing that she told him which ring, because he admits that he wouldn’t have picked out a ring like this one.

The Proposal

Mallory flew up on December 30th, 2011 to visit Adam and his family. Little did she know that he had big plans for her. They ate at the Olive Garden before continuing on to the Kansas City Plaza. There Adam had arranged for his friend, Anna McBroom, to pose as a photographer hoping to “build her portfolio”. She offered to give them a free carriage ride in exchange for some romantic couple pictures. It was there in front of the Cinderella carriage that he proposed! After his proposal in front of a crowd of applauding onlookers, they enjoyed a romantic carriage ride for two around the beautifully lit plaza. View the pictures

Another Surprise

After they got off the carriage, Adam drove her up to Crown Center to take some more pictures in front of the Christmas lights. Adam took pictures and slowly raised the camera because he knew what was behind them. Mallory noticed that the camera kept getting higher and higher, and asked, “Adam Booth, what are you taking pictures of??”. She turned around and screamed! It was both of their families standing behind them! Mallory’s whole family had made the 8.5 hour drive up to be a part of this wonderful occasion. View the pictures
